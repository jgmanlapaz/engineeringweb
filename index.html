Learning Electricity.

Mankind has experienced an inflection point in evolutionary progress when they started harnessing the power of electrical energy. Through excellent scientific effort, electricity set the spark (pun intended) for the unprecedented rise of human innovation.


Learning Electronics.

Since the discovery of semiconductors and the subsequent invention of the transistor, mankind has relied on the continuous development of electronics to perform tasks unimaginable in the distant past. This inspired me to commit to learn and understand the fascinating nature of electronics and contribute to the growing field.
