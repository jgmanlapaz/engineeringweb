About Me

My full name is Juan Glicerio Manlapaz and I go by simply as JG. I am currently a junior in Ateneo de Manila University taking Electronics Engineering as a major. I switch places from Quezon City and Olongapo City which are about 250 kilometers apart. A proud son, brother, student-scholar, and Filipino.
